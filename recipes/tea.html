Smooth Sweet Tea

Description

This sweet tea is a classic Southern drink perfect for hot summer days!

Ingridients

  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 6 tea bags
  • Â¾ cup white sugar
  • 6 cups cool water

Steps

  1. Sprinkle a pinch of baking soda into a 64-ounce, heat-proof glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water and add tea bags. Cover and allow to steep for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove tea bags and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water; refrigerate until cold, about 3 hours.
